ABOUT TELANGANA

Being one of the newest states of India, Telangana provides sample opportunity for ardent travellers to explore. Historically speaking, Telangana was ruled by multiple dynasties; hence you can expect to witness some of the historical architectural marvels.

TELANGANA'S HISTORICAL PLACES

Some of the famous places in Telangana include natural beauties, lakes, and zoological parks. Read this article until the end to learn about these places.

CHARMINAR

The Charminar is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Constructed in 1591, the landmark is a symbol of Hyderabad and officially incorporated in the emblem of Telangana.

GOLCONDA FORT

Golconda Fort Hyderabad is an outstanding example of brilliant engineering and magical architecture. Located at a distance of 11km from the city, it is the most famous of all Hyderabad tourist places.

RAMAPPA TEMPLE

The Ramappa temple finds its hold on a 6 feet high platform on a cruciform plan. The temple's chamber is crowned with a shikharam and is surrounded by pradakshinapatha.

MEDHAK CHURCH

The Medak Cathedral, officially known as the Cathedral Church of Saint Mary the Virgin, is a prominent Christian church located in Medak, a town in the Indian state of Telangana.

BIRLA MANDIR

Birla Mandir is a Hindu temple built on a 280 feet (85 m) high hillock called Naubath Pahad on a 13 acres (53,000 m ) plot in Hyderabad, Telangana, India

MUCCA MASJID

Makkah Masjid or Mecca Masjid, is a congregational mosque in Hyderabad, India. It is the largest mosque in the city, and one of the largest in the country, with a capacity of 10,000 people.

SALAR JUNG MUSEUM

Popularly known for its one of the largest one-man collection of the world, Salar Jung Museum is one of the most famous Hyderabad sightseeing places that must be visited
